## Why

After the core game, content, records, and builds are stable, the experience should feel cleaner and more satisfying without changing the core rules. This change collects visual, feedback, and usability polish as a separate slice.

## What Changes

- Improve colors, typography, spacing, and transitions.
- Add optional sound hooks if desired later.
- Improve pause, level-clear, game-over, and active-effect feedback.
- Add difficulty tuning and small quality-of-life adjustments.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `arkanoid-polish`: Presentation, feedback, tuning, and usability polish that does not redefine core gameplay.

### Modified Capabilities

None.

## Impact

- May add assets such as fonts, sprites, or sounds.
- May tune existing constants while preserving established requirements.
