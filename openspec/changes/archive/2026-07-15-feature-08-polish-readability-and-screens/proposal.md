## Why

After builds, baseline sounds, and the expanded level pack are in place, the first polish pass should make the game easier to read and understand. This change narrows polish to the screens and text players see before deeper feedback and quality-of-life tuning.

## What Changes

- Improve colors, contrast, typography, and HUD readability.
- Improve menu, pause, level-clear, and game-over presentation.
- Add concise in-game control hints where they are needed.
- Ensure text fits at the default game window size.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `arkanoid-readability-polish`: Readable primary screens, HUD, controls hints, and presentation polish that does not redefine core gameplay.

### Modified Capabilities

None.

## Impact

- May tune presentation constants and text layout.
- May add lightweight font or UI assets only if they improve readability.
- May tune presentation constants while preserving established gameplay requirements.
