## Why

After builds, baseline sounds, and the expanded level pack are in place, the experience should feel cleaner and more satisfying without changing the core rules. This change collects visual, readability, and usability polish as a separate slice.

## What Changes

- Improve colors, typography, spacing, and transitions.
- Improve pause, level-clear, game-over, and active-effect feedback.
- Add small quality-of-life adjustments based on play observations.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `arkanoid-polish`: Presentation, feedback, tuning, and usability polish that does not redefine core gameplay.

### Modified Capabilities

None.

## Impact

- May add assets such as fonts, sprites, or small visual effects.
- May tune presentation constants while preserving established gameplay requirements.
